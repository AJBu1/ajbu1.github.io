Department of Mathematics, Rutgers University

This page is being updated as I find my old solutions.

This page includes my solutions to some problems on the Rutgers University written qualifying exams. These are from when I was studying for the written quals, so there may be some errors and incomplete solutions. I mainly focused on algebra and did not study real analysis very much. I passed all three exams in August of 2019, so there are no solutions after that point.

If you are studying for the quals, I highly recommend trying to do the problems yourself before viewing my solutions.
