Welcome!

I am a sixth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Mathematics at Rutgers University. My primary research interests are discrete mathematics, experimental mathematics and computer-assisted proving, and algorithmic algebraic geometry. I am working with Doron Zeilberger.

As a high schooler, I began to realize how much of an impact my teachers had on me and have wanted to become a teacher ever since. As I gained more teaching experience, my passion for teaching grew. For more information about my professional devlopment, click here.

I am committed to creating a welcoming and supportive environment for students. I have been the president of GRADient, the LGBTQ professional and graduate student association at Rutgers University, since June 2020. During this time, I successfully applied to make GRADient a recognized Graduate Student Organization.
